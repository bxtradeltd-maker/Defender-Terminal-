Tick duration (applies to every strategy)
One shared setting for live, dry-run, AND shadow trades across every strategy - sets the real options contract length and how many actual ticks a dry-run/shadow entry waits before it's scored. Only affects trades opened after you apply it.
Signal strictness
Adaptive Confluence: every regime check must agree. Confluence: all 4 indicators must agree. RSI/SMA, EMA cross, and Stochastic+BB are already all-or-nothing, so this has no effect on them.
Auto-switch to best strategy
"Decisively" means the candidate's 95% confidence interval for win rate is entirely above the live strategy's - not just numerically higher today. A strategy needs at least the trade count below before it's even considered, which protects against switching to something that only looks good on a small sample.
Stake sizing mode
Sizes each trade off the LIVE strategy's own record, using the statistically conservative (Wilson lower-bound) win rate - not the raw number - so a lucky small sample can't inflate the stake. Falls back to the base stake below the minimum trade count, or if the edge doesn't clear breakeven at all.
SMC gate (per-symbol, adaptive_confluence only)
Only touch based on real smc_sample_rate_check.py evidence for that specific symbol - validated so far: 1HZ25V and 1HZ50V both improve AND clear the 54.05% breakeven line once gated. Everything else either got worse, or improved but stayed unprofitable even gated - turning the gate on there wouldn't make them worth trading. Only applies while adaptive_confluence is the live strategy.
